## Revenue Models

- **Paid downloads** — sell your game on Steam, itch.io, or app stores. The simplest model: players pay once and get the full game.
- **Free-to-play with ads** — common for web and mobile games. **Rewarded ads** (watch an ad for an in-game reward) are generally the most player-friendly ad format.
- **In-app purchases** — sell cosmetics, extra levels, quality-of-life improvements, or bonus content. Cosmetic-only purchases are the least controversial approach.
- **Subscriptions** — recurring payments for ongoing content or benefits.
- **Season passes** — time-limited content bundles that give players goals to work toward.

## Selling PWAs on Stores

You can sell PWAs on native stores: Microsoft allows PWA submissions to the Windows Store, and Google supports wrapping PWAs in a Trusted Web Activity (TWA) for the Play Store (easily done with [PWABuilder](https://www.pwabuilder.com/)). The [Digital Goods API](https://developer.chrome.com/docs/android/trusted-web-activity/receive-payments-play-billing/) enables Play Store billing from a TWA.
