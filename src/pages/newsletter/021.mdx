üá™üá∫ The [**Digital Markets Act**](https://open-web-advocacy.org/blog/the-digital-markets-act-is-in-force-what-happens-now/) is now in force, which means that _gatekeepers_ such as Apple, Google, Meta, and Microsoft must now comply with the new rules. The act aims to create a fair and competitive digital market by preventing gatekeepers from abusing their power. As a reponse to the DMA, Apple tried last week to kill PWAs entirely in the EU and reversed their decision after being met with backlash led by the [Open Web Advocacy](https://open-web-advocacy.org/blog/apple-backs-off-killing-web-apps/).
