## üî• Headlines

Discord launched its [**Embedded App SDK**](https://discord.com/build/embedded-app-sdk) for Activities ([NPM package](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@discord/embedded-app-sdk)). This means multiplayer web games can now be played directly within Discord via iframes. Discord Activities are currently primarily for social multiplayer games with users connected via voice chat. The SDK lets you authenticate players and manage how your game interacts with Discord. Check out the [official starter](https://github.com/discord/embedded-app-sdk/tree/a124ae8eaf51c3fc118059e76af89b051c1c6277/examples/discord-activity-starter), [Hugo Duprez](https://x.com/HugoDuprez)'s [starter](https://github.com/Hugo-Dz/discord-activity-starter), and [Colyseus](https://colyseus.io/)' [starter](https://github.com/colyseus/discord-embedded-app-sdk) ([article](https://colyseus.io/blog/discord-embedded-sdk/)).

Here are the [winners of the Gamedev.js Jam 2024](https://gamedevjs.com/competitions/best-entries-from-all-the-challenges-in-gamedev-js-jam-2024/)! The theme was _Power_.
