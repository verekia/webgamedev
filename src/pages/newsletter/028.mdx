## üî• Headlines

<WebGPULogo /> **WebGPU** support landed in [**Safari 26.0**](https://webkit.org/blog/17333/webkit-features-in-safari-26-0/#webgpu)! And any site can be [installed as a web app](https://webkit.org/blog/17333/webkit-features-in-safari-26-0/#every-site-can-be-a-web-app-on-ios-and-ipados) on iOS and iPadOS.

üò∫ [**Navcat**](https://navcat.dev/) ([repo](https://github.com/isaac-mason/navcat/)) by [Isaac Mason](https://x.com/isaac_mason_) (who made [recast-navigation-js](https://github.com/isaac-mason/recast-navigation-js)) is a new full-featured **navmesh** and **pathfinding** library for your game AI agent needs. Check out the [examples](https://navcat.dev/). Navcat is already used in production in Mana Blade! Isaac is also cooking up an ecosystem of cat-themed libraries for games, with [packcat](https://github.com/isaac-mason/packcat) and [mathcat](https://github.com/isaac-mason/mathcat).

üéæ [**Bounce**](https://perplex.gg/bounce/) ([repo](https://codeberg.org/perplexdotgg/bounce)) by [Perplex](https://x.com/perplexdotgg) (our dear HamzaKubba on [Discord](https://webgamedev.com/discord)) is a new **physics library**. Weighing only 60kb gzipped, it's a batteries-included library with spatial queries, fast raycasting, sweep and intersection tests. It is renderer-agnostic, 100% deterministic, and does not require WebAssembly. Check out the [demos](https://perplex.gg/bounce/#demos) and [API](https://perplex.gg/bounce/#api).

<BabylonLogo /> [**Babylon.js Editor v5**](https://editor.babylonjs.com/) by [Julien Moreau-Mathis](https://x.com/Luaacro) ([repo](https://github.com/BabylonJS/Editor)). Did you know the Babylon.js Editor gives you a Unity-like workflow with Babylon.js? Project data is saved as versionable JSON files. Scripts can be class-based or function-based and attached to your entities. A tool to consider to focus more on creating and less on coding.
