# 🛍 Asset Stores

A collection of stores and libraries where you can find 3D models, textures, animations, and other game assets.

## Characters and Animations

- [Mixamo](https://www.mixamo.com/) — free characters and animations by Adobe

## 3D Models

- [Sketchfab](https://sketchfab.com/)
- [Quaternius](https://quaternius.com/)
- [Kenney](https://kenney.nl/assets) ([particle pack](https://www.kenney.nl/assets/particle-pack), [Twitter](https://x.com/kenneynl))
- [Kay Lousberg](https://kaylousberg.com/game-assets)
- [The Base Mesh](https://thebasemesh.com)
- [Synty](https://syntystore.com/) (paid)
